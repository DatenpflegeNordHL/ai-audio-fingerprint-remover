# Private Beta Privacy Checklist

- This is a private side-project beta.
- It is not publicly advertised.
- Access is restricted by direct subdomain and temporary beta password.
- Cloudflare may process access metadata.
- The home server processes uploaded audio files temporarily.
- Data involved may include IP address, timestamp, user agent, uploaded file, job ID, temporary artifacts, and technical logs.
- Uploads and artifacts are deleted after the configured TTL, initially 24 hours.
- No analytics.
- No public listing.
- No upload or output backups.
- No legal advice claims.
- This beta does not claim platform acceptance, distributor acceptance, or changes to watermark, fingerprint, provenance, detector, C2PA, source-attribution, evasion, or detectability behavior.
